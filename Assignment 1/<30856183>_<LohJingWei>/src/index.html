Frogger

Controls:

Hop to Left
⇦
Hop to Right
⇨
Hop Upward
⇧
Hop Backward
⇩

Instructions:

Frogger can score 1 point by moving forwards

Land on the target box (yellow) on the top row to score 100 points

Avoid colliding with the cars (pink squres) on the road sections

Use the plank (brown rectangle) and crocodile to move across the river

Landing on the crocodile mouth will result in frog death (crocodile eats frog!!)

Frogger starts with 3 lives, colliding with cars and falling into the river will reduce frogger's lives by 1

Consuming the fly (black box) on the river section to get double the points when moving forwards

Gameover when lives is 0

Restart the game anytime you like by pressing spacebar

After landing into all 5 target boxes, difficulty level will increase by 1, the speed of obstacles will increase
